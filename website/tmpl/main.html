Visualize HL7 Example and Test Instances

This site – contributed by HL7 Europe – is a reference implementation of the vi7eti stylesheets that come out of the xShare and Gravitate Health projects. Here we show and demonstrate the Visualization of Example and Test Instances based on HL7 CDA or HL7 FHIR specifications (implementation guides).
